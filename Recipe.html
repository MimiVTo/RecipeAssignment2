Red Velvet Cake

Ingredients for the Cake

  • Cake Flour
  • Unsweetened Coco Powder
  • Butter
  • Oil
  • Buttermilk

Ingredients for the Cream Cheese Frosting

  • Brick-style Cream Cheese
  • Butter
  • Confectioners Sugar
  • A Splash of Milk
  • Vanilla Extract
  • A Pinch of Salt

Optional Toppings or Variations

  • Red Food Coloring--Temporary since it's not necessary unless you really want the red color for your red velvet cake.
  • Cherries--For extra flavor.
  • Sprinkles
